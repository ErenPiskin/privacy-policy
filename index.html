Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the My Emotional Journey app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Eren Piskin (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application collects and stores the following information:

  • Emotion journals and entries you create
  • Dream journals and entries you create
  • Mood tracking data
  • Achievements and progress information
  • App preferences and settings

This information is stored locally on your device by default and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you choose to use certain premium features like backup.

Google Drive Backup Feature

Our Application offers a premium feature to backup your data to your personal Google Drive account. When you use this feature:

  • Your emotion journals, dream journals, and related app data are encrypted and uploaded to your Google Drive account's AppData folder
  • This data is only accessible by you and our application with your explicit permission
  • We do not have access to this data on our servers
  • You can enable automatic backups or perform manual backups at your discretion
  • You can delete your backups at any time

What permissions does the Application require?

For the Google Drive backup feature, our app requests the following permissions:

  • drive.appdata: To store and retrieve your app data in a dedicated folder
  • drive.file: To manage files created by the app

These permissions are only used for backup and restore functionality and are not used to access any other files in your Google Drive.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Device Information

For app functionality and crash reporting, we may collect:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and installation ID
  • Error logs in case of app crashes

This information is used solely for troubleshooting and improving app performance and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Premium Purchases and Payment Information

When you purchase premium features in our Application:

  • Payment processing is handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information such as credit card details
  • Your purchase information (subscription status, renewal dates) may be stored locally to verify your access to premium features
  • For subscription management, please visit your device's app store account settings

Free vs Premium Features and Data Limitations

Our Application offers both free and premium tiers with different data handling capabilities:

  • Free Version Limitations:
    • Limited to 10 emotion journal entries and 10 dream journal entries
    • Limited access to analytics features
    • Access to only basic emotion cards
    • Cannot back up data to Google Drive
    • Limited to 3 daily activities
  • Premium Version Features:
    • Unlimited emotion and dream journal entries
    • Full access to detailed emotional analytics
    • Access to all emotion cards
    • Ability to back up and restore data through Google Drive
    • Access to up to 5 daily activities

Your personal data is handled with the same level of security and privacy protection regardless of whether you are using the free or premium version. The limitations are only on the volume of data that can be stored and the features available, not on how we protect your information.

Push Notifications

Our Application may send you push notifications with reminders and motivational messages. You can:

  • Enable or disable notifications through the app settings or your device settings
  • Customize notification frequency and types within the Application
  • We do not use notifications for marketing purposes outside the scope of the Application

We do not collect or analyze notification interaction data on our servers.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

When you use the Google Drive backup feature, your data is stored on Google's servers according to Google's privacy policy. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

Data Storage and Deletion

  • All data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Backups stored on Google Drive remain there until you manually delete them
  • Uninstalling the app will remove data from your device but will not automatically delete Google Drive backups
  • You can delete your Google Drive backups through the app's interface or directly from your Google Drive account

Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access the data stored about you
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability (exporting your data)
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at epstudios.dev@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

For Google Drive backups, you can:

  • Disable the automatic backup feature in the app settings
  • Remove the app's access to your Google Drive through your Google Account settings
  • Delete any existing backups from your Google Drive

Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • When using the backup feature, your data is encrypted before being uploaded to Google Drive
  • We use secure data transmission protocols
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information
